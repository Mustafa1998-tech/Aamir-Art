About

The Artisan
Behind the Work

I am a furniture designer and craftsman based in Saudi Arabia, with a deep passion for minimalist forms and honest materials. Every piece I create begins as a conversation between function and beauty.

With over a decade of experience working with solid wood, steel, and natural stone, I believe furniture should not only fill a space — it should define it. My work is an ongoing exploration of proportion, texture, and the quiet poetry of everyday objects.

Each commission is approached with meticulous attention to detail, from the first sketch to the final hand-finish. I collaborate closely with architects, interior designers, and private clients to realize furniture that is both timeless and deeply personal.
